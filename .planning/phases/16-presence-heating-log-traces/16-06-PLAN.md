<objective>
Implement D-03/D-04/D-09: a new `zone.py` module with `Zone` and `ZoneMode`
(plain base class, `assert False` overloads — NOT ABC) plus three concrete
subclasses: `ZoneModeOff`, `ZoneModeTimeProgram`, `ZoneModeProgramPresences`.
ZoneMode uses a `weakref.ref[Zone]` and reads `zone.time_program`, `zone.rooms`
at call time — no config on the mode.

`Zone.evaluate(ctx)` delegates to `self._mode.evaluate(ctx)`. The zone INFO log
(OBS-01) fires only when `(period, mode_name)` changes, formatted per D-03 as
`state=<old_period>[<old_mode>]→<new_period>[<new_mode>]` with the D-09 reason
field (`time_program:normal→22:00`, `user:time_program→off`,
`presence:override`). Anti-spam via `_current_period`/`_current_mode_name`
(D-10). First-tick log is suppressed (RESEARCH Open Question 1 — avoid
`None[None]→...`).

Purpose: Final domain leaf — consumes Room and Person; coordinator integrates it
in 16-07.
