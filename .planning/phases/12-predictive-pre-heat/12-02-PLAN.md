<objective>
Add the predictive pre-heat engine to the coordinator: a new `_async_preheat()`
pass (mirroring `_async_calibrate()`), per-room next-transition computation via
`next_occupied_at()`, inertia sample learning persisted to a new
`climate_manager_preheat` Store, the three new status fields on each room
payload, and the per-person `preheat_lead_minutes` → `wakeup_advance_minutes`
read-site rename in coordinator.py.

Decisions implemented here: D-04 (coordinator takes the earliest non-None
next_occupied_at across assigned persons), D-06 (per-room samples in
Store(hass, "climate_manager_preheat") as {duration_minutes, timestamp}),
D-07 (valid-sample / invalid-discard rule), D-08 (learned lead = avg of last 5
valid samples capped at max; 60 min until 3 samples), D-09 (in-memory
_preheat_in_progress + convergence record / pre-period discard), and D-10
(room state adds preheat_active / preheat_target / preheat_suppressed).

Purpose: This is the behavioral core of Phase 12 — it turns the next-transition
helper and constants from Wave 1 into actual early-heating actions and learned
lead times, and surfaces live pre-heat status to the panel (consumed by Wave 3).

Output: `_async_preheat()` / `_async_preheat_room()` / convergence + sample
recording (PREHEAT-02, PREHEAT-03), status fields on the room payload
(PREHEAT-04), the second Store wired into `ClimateManagerData`, and coordinator
read-site rename for D-02.
